Our Story

(More...)

The Seattle Chinese Volleyball Club (SCVC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the sport of volleyball within the Chinese community and beyond. Our mission is to provide opportunities for individuals of all skill levels to learn, play, and excel in volleyball. Through inclusive programs, competitive events, and community outreach, we aim to inspire a love for the game and create lasting connections among our members in the greater Seattle area.
